HealthNote

HealthNote is a healthcare app made for discharged heart disease patients. This app is empowered by IBM Watson's natural language processing, which allows us to combine and process data from medical journals, patients' record, and doctor's notes to provide personalized answers for patients.

Our initial target market was India, becuase Apollo Hospital, the biggest hospital chain in India agreed to provide us useful medical data. There's also less healthcare regulation in India which allows us more freedom to create the app.

This app is developed during an independent course at UC Berkeley in partnership with IBM Watson group.

While initially we wanted to make this product a place for general healthcare questions, we later decided to focus on discharged heart disease patients because we wanted to focus more on attacking the problem of high readmission rate for heart disease patients.

Client: IBM Watson, UC Berkeley - Type: App Design - Date: Dec 2014
