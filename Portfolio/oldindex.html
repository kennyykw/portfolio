Happiness

An app that makes people happy.

Little Things in Life

We are much happier if we appreciate little things in life, everyday.

Positive Reinforcement

Psychological effect of actually writing down how you are grateful of little things in life reinforces positive emotions.

More Expressive

Enhancing your gratitude statement with pictures makes it more expressive, shareable, and delightful.

Happy Color

Happy #ef4163 gradient background creates feelings of warmth, gratitude, and compassion throughout the entire experience.

Sharing is Loving

Spread across social networks and generate a huge network effect of happiness.

Simple as 123

Three-step app. Takes less than 2 minutes of your life everyday, so you can focus more on things that make you happier.

Forming a Habit

The goal of this app is to help people form a habit of appreciating little things everyday. I used the "Trigger-Behavior-Rewards" habit forming model as a base concept.
