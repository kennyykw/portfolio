The product owners insisted they wanted to have an "above the fold" design because they believed it's easier for users and looks distinguished from other services such as Linkedin, so we moved on with this direction.

After putting much focus on making recruiters job easier, we realized that it's also important to have a design that allows candidates to show off their personalities, so I experimented with some "softer" look and feel, and added more personal branding options. We also wanted to have a universal access of candidate's contact information available for recruiters.
