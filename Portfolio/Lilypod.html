Some early iterations..

Trying to make a fancy "post-it-note-peel-effect, but later on realized this is too much and not worth the development time.

While initially I thought having a big photo of the caregiver will boost emotional connections among collaborators, I realized for most people they should already have a lot of close communication outside of the app, and a photo shouldn't be needed for reminding who's taking care of the baby.


Knowing this, I tried out some more simplied iterations and put more focus on the activity and time.

After some design reviews with product owners, they decided that they liked the timeline look and feel of the following design.

Client: Lilypod - Type: UX Design - Date: Dec 2014
